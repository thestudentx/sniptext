Roman numeral rules

  • I, V, X, L, C, D, M with subtractive pairs like IV, IX, XL, XC, CD, CM in strict mode.
  • Repeat I, X, C, M up to 3 times. V, L, D never repeat in strict mode.
  • Extended mode uses an overline to denote ×1000. Example: V = 5000.
