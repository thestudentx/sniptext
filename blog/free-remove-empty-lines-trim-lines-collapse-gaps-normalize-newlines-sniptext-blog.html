Free Remove Empty Lines: Trim Lines, Collapse Gaps, Normalize Newlines

Paste or drop text and remove empty lines in one click. Keep one clean paragraph break if you want, trim each line, and output LF or CRLF for stable diffs. Perfect for markdown, CSV, logs, and code.

Clean spacing makes content easier to scan and maintain. The SnipText Remove Empty Lines tool deletes blank lines, collapses long gaps, trims each line, and normalizes newlines. It also catches invisible troublemakers like zero width spaces and BOM so odd wrapping and cursor jumps stop for good. Everything runs locally in your browser.

What the Remove Empty Lines tool does

  • Delete or keep gaps: Remove all empty lines or keep 1 to 3 in a row for readable paragraph breaks.
  • Trim per line: Strip leading and trailing spaces on each line for tidy diffs and commits.
  • Normalize newlines: Output as LF \n or CRLF \r\n to match team settings and stop EOL churn.
  • Handle zero width and BOM: Treat \u200B to \u200D and \uFEFF as spaces to fix hidden junk.
  • Regex controls: Mark lines as empty with a pattern or protect lines you never want removed.
  • Quality of life: Paste, import, drag and drop, copy, download, share link, live preview, and a quick keyboard shortcut.
  • Stats panel: See input lines, output lines, and how many blanks were removed.

How to use it fast

  1. Open Remove Empty Lines.
  2. Paste text into Input or drop a file.
  3. Choose how many consecutive blank lines to keep. Set 0 to remove every empty line.
  4. Toggle Trim each line and pick newline format LF or CRLF.
  5. (Optional) Add regex in Treat as empty or Never remove to fine tune behavior.
  6. Press Ctrl or Cmd + Enter to run, then Copy or Download.
  • Markdown flow: Keep 1 empty line so paragraphs stay clear without big gaps.
  • CSV cleanup: Enable trim per line to remove stray spaces that break parsing.
  • Protect comments: Use Never remove with ^\s*\/\/ or ^\s*# to keep comment blocks.
  • Remove separators: Use Treat as empty with ^[\.\-]+$ to delete dotted or dashed rules.
  • Team EOL rules: Pick LF for Unix and CRLF for Windows so your diffs stop flipping.
  • Hidden junk: Turn on zero width and BOM handling to fix odd cursor jumps and copy bugs.

Why remove empty lines

Extra blank lines bloat files, break folding, and hide real changes in diffs. A quick cleanup improves readability, keeps previews steady in CMS and README files, and avoids noisy merges in Git and CI.

FAQ

Is the Remove Empty Lines tool free
Yes. No sign in and no limits for normal use.
Do you store my text
No. It runs in your browser for a private and fast workflow.
How is this different from Whitespace and Formatting Cleaner
This tool targets empty lines and simple trimming. The cleaner also removes trailing spaces, converts tabs to spaces, handles BOM and zero width, and more. Use both as needed.
Can I keep one blank line between paragraphs
Yes. Set Keep consecutive empty lines to 1.
Can I force LF or CRLF
Yes. Pick the newline format in the dropdown to match your project.
Does it support regex
Yes. Use Treat as empty to target lines by pattern and Never remove to protect lines. Examples: ^[\.\-]+$ for rule lines, ^\s*\/\/ for code comments.
