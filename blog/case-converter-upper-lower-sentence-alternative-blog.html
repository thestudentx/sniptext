What you can convert

  • UPPERCASE: Ideal for headings, short labels, and emphasis.
  • lowercase: Normalize usernames, hashtags, or tags.
  • Sentence case: Automatically capitalizes after punctuation marks.
  • Title Case: Formats academic titles and blog headings while respecting words like of, and, the. For rules, see the APA title case guide. See common pitfalls in Top Grammar Blunders.
  • Alternating case: Fun casing for memes, creative writing, or playful text.
