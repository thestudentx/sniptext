Free Palindromic Word Finder: Detect, Highlight, and Export Palindromes

Detect palindromic words, inner substrings, and full phrases across spaces and punctuation. See positions, highlight in context, and export CSV or JSON for QA, linguistics, and content audits.

Palindromes read the same forward and backward, like level or racecar, and phrases such as never odd or even. Beyond wordplay, they surface symmetry patterns that matter for QA testing, linguistics, search and tokenization, and UX copy checks. The SnipText Palindromic Word Finder scans in real time, highlights results, and outputs structured data you can reuse.

What the tool finds

  • Palindromic words: Tokens like madam, refer, level.
  • Palindromic substrings: Inner sequences inside words, for example anna in Banana.
  • Palindromic phrases: Sentence level detection across spaces and punctuation.
  • Exact positions: Start index, end index, and length for each match.
  • De duplication: Optional unique only listing to reduce noise in large texts.

How to use it

  1. Open the Palindromic Word Finder.

  3. Choose mode: Words, Substrings, or Phrases.
  4. Set options: Ignore case, Ignore spaces, Ignore punctuation, Ignore diacritics.
  5. Export results as CSV or JSON, or Copy Report for tickets and docs.
