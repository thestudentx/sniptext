Free Whitespace and Formatting Cleaner: Trim, Normalize, Fix Indentation

Paste or type your text to strip extra spaces, remove trailing whitespace, normalize LF or CRLF line endings, collapse blank lines, convert tabs to spaces, and remove zero width characters and BOM. One click cleanup for docs, markdown, and code.

When text travels through emails, docs, editors, and CMSs it picks up mess. Mixed line endings, stray tabs, trailing spaces, and invisible characters can break layouts and diffs. The SnipText Whitespace and Formatting Cleaner restores clean, consistent formatting that is ready to publish, submit, or commit.

What the cleaner fixes

  • Trim whitespace: Remove leading and trailing spaces, plus per line trailing whitespace.
  • Normalize newlines: Convert everything to LF or CRLF and fix mixed endings.
  • Collapse blanks: Reduce multiple blank lines to a single break or your chosen count. For gap control use Remove Empty Lines.
  • Indentation: Convert tabs to spaces or spaces to tabs and normalize indent size for clean alignment.
  • Invisible junk: Strip zero width characters and optional BOM U+FEFF. Inspect invisibles or code points with Symbol and Character Info.
  • Preview invisibles: Show spaces, tabs, and newlines to spot issues quickly.

How to use it fast

  1. Open the Whitespace and Formatting Cleaner.
  2. Paste or type. Preview updates in real time as you change options.
  3. Pick line ending (LF or CRLF), choose indentation style and size, and enable trim options.
  4. Click Clean All then Copy or Download the result.
  • Match the target: CRLF for Windows docs, LF for UNIX and most repos. Set this once and keep it consistent.
  • Normalize before diffing: Clean whitespace first so code diffs and similarity checks focus on real changes. For batch gap fixes use Remove Empty Lines guide.
  • Tabs to spaces: Standardize indentation to avoid misaligned bullets, code blocks, and tables.
  • Zero width proofing: ZWSP and friends can break search and URLs. Strip them here, or inspect with the Symbol and Character Info guide.
  • Title and labels: Standardize capitalization with the Case Converter then verify limits in the Word and Character Counter.

Why formatting consistency matters

Messy whitespace causes broken lists, odd wraps, noisy diffs, and flaky tests. Clean formatting improves readability, reduces file size, and prevents platform specific glitches when you publish, submit, or commit.
