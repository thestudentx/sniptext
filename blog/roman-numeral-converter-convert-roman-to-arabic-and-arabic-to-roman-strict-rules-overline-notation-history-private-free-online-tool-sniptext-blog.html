Frequently asked questions

What is strict subtractive notation?
Strict rules allow only specific subtractive pairs (IV, IX, XL, XC, CD, CM) and limit repetitions to keep numerals canonical and unambiguous.
How do overlines work?
An overline multiplies the value by 1000. For example, overlined V equals 5000. In the tool, enable extended mode to output overlined characters.
Can I paste numerals with odd spacing?
Yes-clean them first with the Whitespace & Formatting Cleaner or Remove Empty Lines and try again.
Does the converter accept lowercase?
Yes. Input can be upper or lower case; output is standardized.
