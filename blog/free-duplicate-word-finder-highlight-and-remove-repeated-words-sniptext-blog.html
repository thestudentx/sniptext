Free Duplicate Word Finder: Highlight and Remove Repeated Words

Paste or type your text to instantly highlight repeated words and consecutive duplicates. Clean double words automatically, export a polished version, and keep your writing crisp for resumes, emails, essays, and SEO content.

Repeated words slip in when you edit fast or merge sources. SnipText Duplicate Word Finder flags repeats, detects consecutive duplicates, and removes them in one click so your copy reads cleanly and professionally. Pair it with the Word and Character Counter and the Case Converter to finish on spec.

What the tool finds

  • All repeated words: A frequency list of words used more than once, with a minimum threshold you control.
  • Consecutive duplicates: Instant detection of double words like is is, can can, or the the.
  • Smart filters: Toggle case sensitive, ignore stopwords (the, and, of), and ignore numbers.
  • Live highlight: See duplicates highlighted as you type or paste.

How to use it

  1. Open the Duplicate Word Finder.
  2. Paste your text or start typing and watch repeats highlight instantly.
  3. Switch views to show all repeats or only consecutive duplicates.
  4. Click Remove Consecutive to auto clean double words, then copy or download the result.
