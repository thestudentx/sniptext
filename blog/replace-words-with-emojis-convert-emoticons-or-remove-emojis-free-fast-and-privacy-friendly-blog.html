How to use it fast

  1. Open the Emoji Replacer.
  2. Paste your text or start typing. Preview updates in real time.
  3. Choose Replace Words, Convert Emoticons, or Remove Emojis.
  4. Add dictionary lines and set whole word, case match, skin tone, and exclusions.
  5. Click Apply, then Copy or Download.
  • Brand voice: Limit to 1 to 2 emojis per sentence for professional channels.
  • Protect proper nouns: Add product names and people to Exclusions.
  • Consistency: Enable variant normalization so emojis look the same across devices.
  • Accessibility: For documents read by screen readers, consider Remove emojis or add alt text in the surrounding copy.
